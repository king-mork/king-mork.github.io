Professional Career...

I received my Bachelors in Computer Science from the beautiful University of Victoria in 2021. During my time at UVic, I got the opportunity to do an 8 month work-term with CGI, an IT consulting firm wherein I got work as a QA Analyst. My primary responsibilities were concerned with the writing of automated test cases and the further development of our Selenium-based testing framework.

The majority of my career so far has been spent as a software dev at a VFX and animation company: DNEG. I spent more than two and a half years developing and maintaining various tools and applications that served an instrumental role in DNEG's production pipeline. Initially I was part of a Python 3 conversion team, but after a few months I stepped into the role of a developer in Core Services. Aside from maintaining core libraries and expanding the usage of GraphQL services/APIs, it was here that I built my first web application. The application in question was a UI that rendered an interative, hierarchical graph that visualized assets and their relation to one another.
