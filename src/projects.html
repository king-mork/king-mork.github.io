Gasoline Alley

Hey! I'm making a game!

This project is a demo for a game that I've wanted to make since my university days. Inspired by the classic Resident Evil games, the player will find themselves stranded in a remote town in the mountains where the inhabitants have become feral creatures that attack on sight. Gameplay will involve exploring the sparse buildings and solving puzzles with the ultimate goal of escaping the town while also defending themselves from the residents of the town with limited resources.

I'm developing this project in Unreal Engine 5. The core mechanics are still being implemented, but once it is in a shareable state I'll post a link here!
